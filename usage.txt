Usage tutorial:

For use AngryScanner you will write following commands:

python AngryScanner.py -host [IP or Web] -min [min port number] -max [max port number]

And wait for scanning open & close ports on host entry.

Thanks for using (AngryScanner) :)

[This script can find open & close ports on (Host or WebLinks) to scan target [vulnerable] or not]
